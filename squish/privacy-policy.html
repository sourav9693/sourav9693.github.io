Privacy Policy for Squish

Effective date: 2026-07-16

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Squish ("the app," "we," "our") handles information when you use the game on your Apple device. Squish is developed and published by Sourav Mishra.

By using Squish, you agree to the practices described here.

What we collect

Squish does not require you to create an account and does not ask for personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number.

The app collects the following information automatically through third-party SDKs:

  • Advertising identifier (IDFA) — used by Google AdMob to serve advertisements.
  • Device information — device model, operating system version, and general system settings, used by Google AdMob for ad delivery and performance measurement.
  • App usage data — ad impressions, ad clicks, and anonymous crash diagnostics (via Google AdMob).

We do not collect:

  • Location data
  • Contacts, photos, or files on your device
  • Health, fitness, or biometric data
  • Any personally identifying information

In-app purchases

Squish offers a one-time in-app purchase to remove ads. Transactions are processed by Apple through the App Store; we do not receive or store your payment information. Apple's privacy practices apply to those transactions.

How we use information

The information collected via the SDKs above is used to:

  • Serve and measure advertisements through Google AdMob
  • Detect and diagnose crashes to improve the app
  • Verify in-app purchases through Apple StoreKit

We do not sell your data. We do not use it for any purpose beyond those listed.

Third-party services

Squish uses the following third parties:

  • Google AdMob — for advertising. See Google's privacy practices at policies.google.com/privacy.
  • Apple StoreKit — for in-app purchases. See Apple's privacy practices at apple.com/legal/privacy.

Your choices

You can opt out of personalized advertising at any time in iOS Settings:

  • Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking — disable "Allow Apps to Request to Track" for Squish (or globally).
  • Settings → Privacy & Security → Apple Advertising — turn off personalized ads system-wide.

Purchasing the "Remove Ads" in-app product disables ad-related data collection entirely on that install.

Children's privacy

Squish is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided information through the app, contact us and it will be deleted from any records we hold.

Data retention

We do not operate any servers that store personal information. All data collection described above happens client-side through third-party SDKs and is retained according to those providers' policies (Google, Apple).

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy over time. The "Effective date" at the top will reflect the most recent revision. Material changes will be announced in a future app update.
